## Who Am I?

I'm Ilia Agafonov (a.k.a. Tairesh), originally from Yekaterinburg and currently living in Batumi, Georgia. He/him. Rust, PHP, and Python developer, and a Web4.0 enthusiast.

I enjoy chess, roguelike and strategy games, space and astronomy, sci-fi stuff, and open-source software. Recently, I became a father. Now I'm trying to be a good parent, improve my cooking skills, and find my place in the world.
